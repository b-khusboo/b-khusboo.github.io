About me

As a firm believer of “Learning before knowing”, I continuously urge to augment my skills. My academic coursework at UT Dallas and current internship as a Data Analyst introduced me to a whole new gamut of technologies thus laying a strong base for my fundamentals. Prior to this internship, I worked as a Decision Scientist helping enterprises systematize better data-driven decision making. Both my work experiences demanded me to be a quick learner and a performer driven with good communication skills and strong analytical skills equipped to manage team and client relationships.
Having gained an intensive industry experience for almost 2 years, I developed an aptitude for art of problem solving and a structured approach towards Exploratory Data Analysis. At school, I try to improve my learning curve with regards to leadership and communication skills by participating and volunteering for social/ academic events. With a passion to become a data-nerd, I aspire to be able to come to work every day to a place where I know not only are my technical skills valuable, but my personal philosophies are as well.

The University of Texas at Dallas

Organizational Behaviour

  • As part of this project, me and my colleagues were consulted as part-time management consultants by a growing startup to provide solutions for their problems.

  • Being a growing startup, the company was yet to set up standard working procedures and majority of the workforce was short-term interns (paid/unpaid).

  • With learning curve involved with each new batch of interns and little to no documentation of work so far due to fast paced development, the primary problems faced by the client were:
    • Visibility of work
      • How to efficiently enable work transparency across the organization?
    • Knowledge Management
      • What is the most effective way of creating, sharing, using and managing information across the organization?
    • Motivation
      • How to keep the employees motivated?

  • To solve these problems, we talked to various employees and founders of the company and came up with suggested solutions for each of the problems.
